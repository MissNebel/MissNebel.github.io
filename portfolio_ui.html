OddOgle

OddOgle was the outcome of TiCorporate 5, a school project in JAMK. We had four students in OddOgle development team. In this project I worked as UI Designer, Front-end Developer and Product Owner. OddOgle was developed with Angular 4, Ionic 2, NodeJS, Express and MongoDB.

OddOgle was a social media platform where users could share their snapshots and wonder the interesting aspects of the world together. OddOgle was meant to be a platform for sharing the real world without beautifying so filters and such were not available. It was also not possible to upload pictures from the gallery: only straight from the camera app. OddOgle was published as beta in Google Play.

Beyond the Eclipse

Beyond the Eclipse was a small game development project in Multidisciplinary Concept Lab course. Our final task was to design a game concept and create a little prototype. I designed UI parts and created a little usable demo of UI functionals with InVision.

Eat your Greens

Other part of Concept Lab course was Design Thinking. The task was to get familiar with the design thinking process and methods by ideating gamified app and creating a little prototype of it. My idea was an app to help people to get healthier by eating more plant based food.
